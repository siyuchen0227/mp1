My Photography Journey

My urban exploration journey began 7 years ago when I first stepped into an abandoned subway station in Berlin. That moment sparked my passion for documenting the forgotten corners of our cities and the stories they hold.

I specialize in urban exploration, architectural photography, and street documentation. My approach combines respect for historical preservation with artistic vision, creating images that honor both the past and present of urban spaces.

My Story

I'm an urban exploration photographer with 7 years of experience capturing the soul of cities worldwide. From abandoned buildings to bustling street corners, I find beauty in the forgotten and overlooked spaces that tell our urban stories.
